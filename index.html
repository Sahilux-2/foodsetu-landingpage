Two Crises. One Opportunity.

Every day, perfectly edible food is discarded while communities nearby struggle to access nutritious meals. The challenge is not availability—it's connection.

Food Waste

Restaurants, caterers, and college messes generate surplus food daily due to overproduction, event leftovers, and demand fluctuations.

  • Significant edible food discarded
  • Lost nutritional value
  • Environmental impact
The Missing Link

No reliable, fast system to connect surplus food with those who need it.

Food Insecurity

NGOs, community kitchens, and shelters depend on unpredictable donations to serve growing demands from local communities.

  • Unpredictable food supply
  • Resource constraints
  • Time spent searching

The Opportunity

By creating a trusted and coordinated donation ecosystem, surplus food can be redirected to communities instead of landfills. Food Setu exists to bridge this gap.
