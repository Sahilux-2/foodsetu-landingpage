Two Crises. One Opportunity.

Every day, perfectly edible food is discarded while communities nearby struggle to access nutritious meals. The challenge is not availability—it's connection.

Food Waste

Restaurants, caterers, and college messes generate surplus food daily due to overproduction, event leftovers, and demand fluctuations.

  • Significant edible food discarded
  • Lost nutritional value
  • Environmental impact
The Missing Link

No reliable, fast system to connect surplus food with those who need it.

Food Insecurity

NGOs, community kitchens, and shelters depend on unpredictable donations to serve growing demands from local communities.

  • Unpredictable food supply
  • Resource constraints
  • Time spent searching

The Opportunity

By creating a trusted and coordinated donation ecosystem, surplus food can be redirected to communities instead of landfills. Food Setu exists to bridge this gap.

From Surplus Food to Delivered Meals

Food donation shouldn't require endless phone calls. Food Setu brings every stakeholder into a single ecosystem where donations can be shared, accepted, and tracked with ease.

Step 1

Donor Shares Available Food

Restaurants, event organizers, or households quickly upload food type, quantity, and pickup location using the platform.

Step 2

NGOs Receive Instant Alerts

Nearby verified organizations are instantly notified. They can review distance, quantity, and collection timing.

Step 3

NGO Accepts Donation

The NGO confirms collection directly through the platform. No lengthy coordination or repeated phone calls are required.

Step 4

Pickup is Coordinated

Volunteers or logistics partners are assigned when necessary. Real-time updates track the food's journey.

Step 5

Food is Distributed

Meals reach communities safely. Donors receive confirmation and impact reports for their contribution.
